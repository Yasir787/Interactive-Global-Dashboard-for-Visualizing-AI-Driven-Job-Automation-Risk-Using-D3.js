AI Creating Jobs vs. Destroying Jobs

Contributors: Chris · Lance · Mohmmad · Saqib · Shreyas · Srijan

AI has taken the world by storm. With the introduction of ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, Google Gemini, and many more, these LLMs will certainly have an impact on the jobs and careers of those who use AI. But does AI help in the creation and evolution of these jobs, or does it take away and automate our jobs. These pages both show how it creates jobs, and how it destroys them, which leaves the decision to you. Click the links below to explore for yourself.

The linked scrollytelling pages walk through our datasets and visualizations using D3.js v7, highlighting where AI expands on human work, and where it replaces it, and what that could mean for future labor markets.
